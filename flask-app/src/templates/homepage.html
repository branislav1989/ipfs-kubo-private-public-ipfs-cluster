Welcome to datahosting.company Storage

This guide explains how to get your credentials and use our two core services: the Public IPFS Pinning Service and the Private IPFS Backup Service.

Step 1: Get Your API Credentials

Generate your unique API credentials with a single command. Save these credentials in a secure password manager immediately.

curl -X POST https://api.datahosting.company/register

This is the only time your `api_secret`, `dashboard_token`, and `ipfs_access_hash` will be shown.

{
  "api_key": "YOUR_UNIQUE_API_KEY",
  "api_secret": "YOUR_UNIQUE_API_SECRET",
  "dashboard_token": "YOUR_UNIQUE_DASHBOARD_TOKEN",
  "ipfs_access_hash": "YOUR_UNIQUE_IPFS_ACCESS_HASH"
}

Note: The `ipfs_access_hash` is used internally to prevent unauthorized access to your files. Keep it secure along with your other credentials.

Step 2: How Billing Works (Crucial Information)

You must add credit before using any services. Your account is created with a zero balance.

Payment Method vs. Account Balance

  • We only accept Bitcoin (BTC) for payments.
  • Your account balance, all service prices, and costs are calculated and displayed in Euros (EUR).
  • When you send a BTC payment, it is converted to EUR at the time of confirmation and added to your account credit balance.

Dashboard

Use your `dashboard_token` to access your secure web portal at https://dashboard.datahosting.company. Here you can find your unique BTC deposit address, view your current EUR balance, and see your billing history.

Service 1: IPFS Kubo Pinning (Private & Public)

This service pins files to IPFS with configurable privacy settings. Choose between private (internal access only) or public (gateway accessible) networks.

Storage Pricing (PREPAID)

Price: €0.07 per GB per month

Applies to: Both Private and Public networks

  • Choose retention period: 1 or 2 months
  • Charged upfront: File_Size × €0.07 × Retention_Months
  • Example: 100 GB for 2 months = 100 × €0.07 × 2 = €14.00
  • Files automatically unpinned after retention period expires

Bandwidth Pricing

FREE: 1 GB per month (shared across all services)

Private Network: €0.02 per GB (after free allowance)

Public Network: €0.10 per GB (after free allowance)

  • Each account receives 1 GB free bandwidth every month
  • Bandwidth resets monthly
  • Charged immediately when files are accessed

How to Pin a File

curl -X POST https://api.datahosting.company/api/pins \
     -H "X-API-KEY: YOUR_API_KEY" \
     -H "X-API-SECRET: YOUR_API_SECRET" \
     -F "file=@/path/to/your/file.jpg" \
     -F "retention_months=1" \
     -F "private=true"

Grace Period & Expiration

If your balance reaches €0:

  • All pins immediately unpinned
  • 7-day grace period begins
  • Add credits within 7 days → pins restored automatically (free)
  • After 7 days → Account and all data permanently deleted

After retention period ends: Files automatically unpinned and deleted.

Service 2: IPFS Cluster Backups (Monthly Billing with Replicas)

This service is for long-term backups with configurable redundancy. Choose 1, 2, or 3 replicas based on your availability needs.

Storage Pricing (Monthly Billing)

Daily Rate: €0.0005125 per GB per day

Monthly Rate: €0.0156 per GB per month (30.4375 days average)

Formula: Storage_GB × Replicas × €0.0005125 × Days_Stored

Replica Options

1 Replica (Standard): Basic protection

2 Replicas (High Availability): 2× cost, survives 1 node failure

3 Replicas (Maximum Redundancy): 3× cost, survives 2 node failures

Pricing Examples for 6,000 GB (Full Month):

  • 1 Replica: 6,000 × 1 × €0.0005125 × 30.4375 = €93.60/month
  • 2 Replicas: 6,000 × 2 × €0.0005125 × 30.4375 = €187.20/month
  • 3 Replicas: 6,000 × 3 × €0.0005125 × 30.4375 = €280.80/month

Partial Month Example (15 days):

  • 1 Replica: 6,000 × 1 × €0.0005125 × 15 = €46.13

Mid-Month Replica Change Example:

  • Days 1-15: 6,000 GB × 1 replica × 15 days = €46.13
  • Days 16-30: 6,000 GB × 3 replicas × 15.4375 days = €141.66
  • Total: €187.79 (prorated by days)

How It Works

  1. Upload backup: No upfront charge, must have positive balance
  2. Dashboard shows estimate: Real-time cost calculation
  3. End of month: Charged for actual days × replicas
  4. Change replicas anytime: Cost prorated by days

How to Create a Backup

curl -X POST https://api.datahosting.company/api/cluster/backup \
     -H "X-API-KEY: YOUR_API_KEY" \
     -H "X-API-SECRET: YOUR_API_SECRET" \
     -F "file=@/path/to/your/archive.zip" \
     -F "replica_count=2" \
     -F "retention_days=365"

Note: retention_days is required. Choose how many days to store (e.g., 30 for 1 month, 365 for 1 year, 730 for 2 years)

Grace Period

If your balance reaches €0:

  • 7-day grace period begins
  • Add credits to continue service
  • After 7 days: All backups, replicas, and account permanently deleted

Please manage your credits and backups responsibly.
